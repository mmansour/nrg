Get Connected to Western

In addition to an unsurpassed financial experience, we offer unique opportunities to meet with you in person. Simply put, we bring Western to you. Here’s a quick look at what we have in place to help you manage your finances.

Loan Sessions

During our Loan Sessions, we’ll be on hand to help you with any loan questions you may have. We’ll even provide personal assistance while you’re submitting your loan application—right then and there if you wish. If you’re interesting in financing a purchase with Western this year, please keep an eye on our calendar for upcoming Loan Sessions.

Benefits Fairs

We can also be there to answer your financial questions during Benefits Fairs. If you want to know more about Western membership or are curious about a specific product or service, we’re here to help. Plus, you can count on us coming loaded with great giveaway items, too! Check our calendar to see when Western will be visiting next.

Lunch and Learns

Western believes that having a good financial education leads to good financial decision making. That’s why we offer FREE Financial Workshops. Some popular topics include:

  • Identity Theft: Who’s in your Wallet?
  • Unlocking the Path to Buying a Home
  • Solving the Mystery of Credit Reporting
  • Financial Peace of Mind: How to Build a Basic Budget
  • The Benefits of Western Federal Credit Union

Keep an eye out for a Lunch and Learn that interests you.
